Welcome to the Sauce Boss App.

Thanks for helping us test Sauce Boss! Please let us know if you encounter any bugs or funny behavior while using the app. "Toss" your friends' some sauce when you want to show them love or "Drop" their sauce when they need to be brought back to earth. They won't know who tossed/dropped them, only that it was a friend. Be thoughtful about what message you want to send your squad because you can only send it one time a day.

App Description

The only app that tells you what your sauce level is! Determined by your friends "tossing" or "dropping" your sauce each day. Keep doing great things and one day your friends might make you the Boss.
